 This post is all about the rocketry froshee experience. Like all cults, sorry clubs,  you needed to have a right of passage, and rocketry had the Tri-Fo competition.
I was in the avionics sub-team with a classmate Renee so we formed a team. Before this I didn't have much wood shop experience so placing third was a lot of grease on my elbow.

Tri-Fo's were done and Renee left Rocketry so I was the only froshee in the avionics team with Luigi, Wintta and Fadi. I actually learnt a lot, like there was a meeting on January 2nd and i was ecstatic and ready to go. I was learning about the use of weather balloons- not to fly myself but for some electronic stuff- and it was awesome.
